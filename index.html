⚡ Metals & Finishes
Gold
14K gold-plated brass. Warm, classic tone.
Rhodium
Silver-tone. Resists tarnish better than silver.
Rose Gold
Soft copper-pink tone. Very popular & feminine.
Gunmetal
Dark charcoal finish. Edgy, modern look.
Gold Vermeil
18K gold over sterling silver. Most durable plating.
14K Solid Gold
Fine jewelry line. Won't tarnish. Higher price point.

Care tip to share with clients: Remove jewelry before swimming, showering, or applying lotion/perfume. Store flat in a soft pouch. Wipe with a soft cloth after wearing.

💍
Hypoallergenic options
Rhodium-plated pieces are a great call for clients with metal sensitivities. Always worth mentioning for new customers unsure about reactions.

💬 Common Objections & Responses
❓
"Is it real gold/silver?"
"Most pieces are gold-plated brass or rhodium-plated — that's what keeps the price accessible while still looking beautiful. We also carry a fine jewelry line in solid 14K gold if you want something that will last generations."
❓
"Will it tarnish?"
"The rhodium-plated pieces resist tarnishing really well. Just keep it away from water, lotion, and perfume and it'll stay beautiful. I tell all my clients to put jewelry on last and take it off first."
❓
"It seems expensive."
"For real semi-precious stones and the quality you're getting, it's actually really accessible — most pieces are under $80. And you're getting a brand that hundreds of celebrities wear and that sells millions of pieces a year."
❓
"I'm not sure which color to pick."
"That's what the Color Bar is for! We can swap the stone right here in store and you can see which one feels most like you. It's one of the best parts of buying Kendra Scott."
